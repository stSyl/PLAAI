## Task:
The AI-Supported Cognitive Development pattern leverages AI to enhance learners' cognitive skills. Personalized learning experiences, adaptive content, and real-time feedback from AI tools enable learners to develop higher-order thinking skills. However, reliance on AI tools may reduce human interaction and could lead to over-dependence on the technology. To address these challenges, use AI tools as a complement rather than a replacement for human interaction, and ensure learners maintain a balance between the use of AI and their own cognitive effort.
