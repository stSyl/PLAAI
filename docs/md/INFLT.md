## Task:
The Information and Media Literacy pattern aids learners in accessing, evaluating, and using information from various sources effectively and ethically. The pattern aims to promote critical thinking, responsible use of media, and informed decision-making. Challenges such as misinformation, biased sources, and plagiarism can be addressed by educating learners about research methodologies, critical evaluation of sources, and proper citation practices.
