## Task:
The Outline pattern is a pre-writing activity that aids students in organizing their thoughts and planning their written work. It aims to improve the clarity and coherence of the final piece. To optimize this pattern, clear instructions, guidance on organization strategies, and the encouragement of revisions based on feedback are crucial.
