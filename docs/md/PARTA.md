## Task:
The Participation pattern advocates active involvement and engagement in the learning process. It aids in creating a dynamic, inclusive learning environment, and can foster a deeper understanding of content. However, achieving this requires setting clear expectations and providing opportunities for engagement that accommodate diverse learning styles and preferences. Constructive feedback encourages student growth within this pattern.
