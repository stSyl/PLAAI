## Task:
PLAAI - a Pattern Language for AI-Augmented Instruction - is a system of structured, reuseable, and composable patterns that support the design and implementation of educational activities. PLAAI's principles are based on a modern understanding of education in various schools and traditions, and on the actual practice in the classroom. Crucially, all patterns address the opportunities and challenges that arise from generative AI resources, and while most patterns do  not strictly depend on AI, the specifics of the many ways that a careful, creative use of AI resources can enhance educational delibvery and outcomes are discussed from a practical perspective.
