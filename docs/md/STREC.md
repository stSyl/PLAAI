## Task:
The Recruitment pattern supports the process of attracting, selecting, and enrolling students into educational programs. This pattern is critical in enrolling a diverse and well-qualified student body into the institution. To make this pattern work well, common issues such as limited reach, biased selection processes, and inadequate support for prospective students should be addressed to create a more equitable and effective recruitment strategy.
